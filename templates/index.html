The simple yet powerful 10 year Investment Plan

Everyone wants to get rich. Everyone dreams of getting rich, fast. A few do, but let's be honest - quite a few of us struggle to achieve our goals.

We are an impatient lot. We miss to undertsand the potential of investing consistently over a longer period of time. The key word here is 'consistent'. For those who do not have time to track the market and can afford to put aside some moeny every week, they can follow this simple but effective plan.

Buy an index etf (my favourite is SPY which tracks the S&P 500 in real-time) or your favourite trustworthy etf available on every major national stock exchange every week for the next 10 years.

Though past performance is no guarantee of future results, it can give us an insight into what we could have achieved if we had followed this plan.
